About the Creator

🌿 House of Crochet is an initiative by Shushama Tripathi. She rediscovered her love for crochet at the age of 66 after nearly 40 years. What once started as a hobby has now become a joyful creative journey. She lovingly creates handmade crochet flowers, bags, and decorative pieces, each crafted with patience and attention to detail. For her, crochet is not just a craft but a source of happiness and peace. Through this small initiative, she hopes to share the warmth of handmade art and spread creativity through every stitch.
