About the Creator

🌿 House of Crochet is an initiative by Shushama Tripathi, Shushama Tripathi is a passionate crochet artist who has rediscovered her love for crochet at the age of 66. After nearly 40 years, she returned to this beautiful craft that once brought her joy, and today it fills her days with creativity and happiness. She lovingly creates handmade crochet flowers, bags, and decorative items, each piece crafted with patience, care, and attention to detail. For her, crochet is not just a hobby but a source of peace and fulfillment. She enjoys exploring new patterns, learning different designs, and turning simple threads into beautiful creations. Through this small initiative, she hopes to share her handmade pieces with others and spread the warmth of handmade art.
